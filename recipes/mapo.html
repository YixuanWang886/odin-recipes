Chinese Mapo Tofu

Description

A simple, delicious Chinese mapo tofu recipe (mabo dobu is the Japanese adaptation) that has been passed on for years.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound soft tofu
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1/2 pound ground pork
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon minced ginger
  • 1 tablespoon fermented black beans, rinsed and minced
  • 1 tablespoon chili bean paste
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onion

Instructions

  1. Drain tofu on paper towels. Cut into 1/2-inch cubes.
  2. Combine chicken broth, soy sauce, hoisin sauce, and cornstarch in a small bowl; set aside.
  3. Heat oil in a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat. Add pork, garlic, ginger, black beans, and chili bean paste; cook and stir until pork is no longer pink, about 5 minutes.
  4. Stir in chicken broth mixture; bring to a boil. Add tofu; cook until heated through, about 5 minutes.
  5. Garnish with green onion.
